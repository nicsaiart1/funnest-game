Fast Tap Challenge

Tap the targets as quickly as possible!

How to Play

Colorful targets will appear on screen. Tap or click them as fast as you can before they disappear!

The faster you tap, the higher your score. Miss too many and it's game over!

Chain hits together quickly to build combos for bonus points!

Look out for special targets and power-ups to enhance your game!
